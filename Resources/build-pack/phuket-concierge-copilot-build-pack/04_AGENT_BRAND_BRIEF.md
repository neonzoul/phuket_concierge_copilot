# Brand / Product Experience Brief

## Positioning
Phuket Concierge Copilot is calm, premium, precise, human-centered, trust-first, and rooted in Phuket hospitality.

## Product Tone
- Calm
- Premium
- Precise
- Human-centered
- Trust-first

## What The UI Must Not Look Like
- generic SaaS
- neon AI
- chatbot template
- heavy dashboard
- tech-first styling

## Core Line
AI handles routine. Humans handle the moments that matter.

## Experience Principles
- Make verified facts feel dependable.
- Make handoff feel safe and clear.
- Make the staff workflow more visible than the chatbot chrome.
- Make the guest experience concise, not verbose.
- Make owner evidence easy to scan.

## Visual Direction
The build should feel like hospitality software, not a consumer AI toy.
Use restrained layout, strong hierarchy, and a calm premium palette.
