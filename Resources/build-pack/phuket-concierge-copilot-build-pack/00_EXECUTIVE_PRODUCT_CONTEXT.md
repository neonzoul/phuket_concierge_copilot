# Executive Product Context

## Product
Phuket Concierge Copilot is a human-centered AI guest care layer for Phuket luxury hospitality.

It is not a general chatbot.
It is a small, reliable system that:
- answers verified routine questions,
- turns requests into structured work,
- stops when human judgment is required,
- creates staff-ready guest context,
- and produces owner-visible evidence.

## Who It Is For
- Guest: wants fast, accurate help without repeating context.
- Staff: wants clear ownership, urgency, and next action.
- Owner / GM: wants pilot evidence, not AI hype.

## Demo Guest
Emma Williams, a UK guest visiting a boutique wellness property in Phuket.

Profile:
- quiet room preference
- gluten-free preference
- sleep and stress recovery goals
- airport transfer request
- massage request
- sensitive concern about high blood pressure and detox safety

## What The Demo Must Prove
1. Routine questions are answered from verified property data.
2. Requests become structured items, not loose chat history.
3. Sensitive questions are routed to humans with context.
4. Staff can read a brief instead of the full conversation.
5. Owners can see measurable evidence from the pilot.

## Trust Threshold
Every message must resolve to one of these states:
- ANSWER
- CONFIRM
- HUMAN
- UNKNOWN

Decision rule:
- HUMAN overrides everything.
- UNKNOWN overrides ANSWER if evidence is insufficient.
- CONFIRM is required for booking, availability, approval, or exception handling.
- ANSWER is allowed only when facts are verified.

## Business Outcome
The product supports hospitality instead of replacing it.

Primary proof line:
AI handles routine. Humans handle the moments that matter.
