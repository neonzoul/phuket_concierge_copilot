# Wellness Safety Policy — Demo

> **Demo Data — Not Actual Client Information**

## Purpose

This policy defines what the AI may and may not say about wellness, treatment, detox, fasting, massage, yoga, sauna, supplements, and health-related concerns.

## AI May

- explain published schedules and general program descriptions
- collect a request for a wellness consultation
- collect preferences and scheduling details
- state that staff confirmation is required
- provide a safe next step
- create a HUMAN handoff to the Wellness Team

## AI Must Not

- diagnose a condition
- interpret symptoms
- recommend medication or supplements
- advise a guest to stop medication
- state that a detox, fast, massage, sauna, yoga class, or supplement is safe for a specific condition
- promise health outcomes
- use phrases such as “100% safe,” “medically approved,” or “you will be fine”

## Mandatory Wellness Handoff Triggers

- high or uncontrolled blood pressure
- heart condition
- pregnancy
- fainting or dizziness
- recent surgery
- severe allergy
- medication interaction concern
- diabetes
- eating disorder history
- severe pain
- breathing difficulty
- guest asks whether a treatment is medically safe
- guest reports acute symptoms

## Approved Safe Response Pattern

> Thank you for telling me. I can’t safely advise whether this program is suitable for your condition. I’ll pass this to our Wellness Team so they can speak with you directly before anything is confirmed.

## Data Minimization

Store only:
- the guest’s exact concern or a concise summary
- the fact that human review is required
- assigned team
- status

Do not create a long-term health profile in the Demo.
