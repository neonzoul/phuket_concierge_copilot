# Nai Harn Wellness Hideaway — Demo Property Handbook

> **Demo Data — Not Actual Client Information**

## 1. Property Overview

Nai Harn Wellness Hideaway is a fictional 18-suite boutique wellness property in the Nai Harn / Rawai area of Phuket.  
It is designed for international guests seeking rest, stress recovery, gentle wellness activities, and thoughtful personal service.

The property is calm and adults-oriented, but guests aged 12 and above are welcome when accompanied by an adult.

## 2. Arrival and Departure

- Standard check-in: 3:00 PM
- Standard check-out: 12:00 PM
- Early check-in: subject to availability and Front Office confirmation
- Late check-out:
  - Until 2:00 PM: 1,500 THB if approved
  - Until 6:00 PM: 50% of the nightly room rate if approved
  - After 6:00 PM: full nightly room rate if approved
- Luggage storage is available free of charge on arrival and departure days.
- A valid passport and a refundable 3,000 THB incidentals authorization are required at check-in.

## 3. Breakfast and Dining

- Breakfast is served daily from 7:00 AM to 10:30 AM at Sati Kitchen.
- Breakfast is included for direct bookings marked as “Breakfast Included.”
- Gluten-free and vegetarian options are available with at least 24 hours’ notice.
- The kitchen cannot guarantee a completely allergen-free environment.
- Room-service breakfast is available from 7:30 AM to 10:00 AM with a 250 THB tray charge.
- Sati Kitchen serves light wellness meals from 11:30 AM to 9:00 PM.
- Last food order: 8:30 PM.

## 4. Facilities

- Quiet Pool: 7:00 AM–8:00 PM
- Wellness Studio: 6:30 AM–8:00 PM
- Treatment Rooms: 9:00 AM–9:00 PM
- Last massage start time: 7:30 PM
- Complimentary Wi-Fi is available throughout the property.
- The property does not have a gym with machines; yoga mats, resistance bands, and light free weights are available in the Wellness Studio.
- There is no lift. Two Garden Calm Suites are step-free and should be requested in advance.

## 5. Wellness Activities

- Morning yoga: 7:30 AM
- Evening restorative yoga: 5:00 PM
- Group classes require reservation and are subject to space.
- Private yoga sessions are available on request.
- Wellness consultations are conducted by a qualified wellness practitioner.
- The AI assistant must never advise whether a detox, treatment, supplement, massage, sauna, or fasting program is medically safe for a specific guest.
- Guests with health conditions, pregnancy, recent surgery, medication use, fainting, uncontrolled blood pressure, or severe allergies must be referred to the Wellness Team.

## 6. House Rules

- Smoking and vaping are not allowed inside suites, restaurants, studios, treatment rooms, or pool areas.
- A designated outdoor smoking point is available near the arrival courtyard.
- Pets are not accepted.
- Quiet hours: 10:00 PM–7:00 AM.
- External visitors must be registered with Front Office.
- Commercial photography requires prior written approval.
- Drone use is not permitted on property without Operations Manager approval.
